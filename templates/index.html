📊 Understanding Soil Parameters

🧪 Nitrogen (N)

Essential for leaf growth and chlorophyll production. Typical range: 50-150 mg/kg

🌟 Phosphorous (P)

Crucial for root development and flowering. Typical range: 20-60 mg/kg

💎 Potassium (K)

Important for disease resistance and fruit quality. Typical range: 100-300 mg/kg

⚖️ pH Level

Affects nutrient availability. Most crops prefer 6.0-7.5 pH range

🌡️ Temperature

Influences crop growth rate and development. Varies by crop type
